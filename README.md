# 🍽️ Restaurant Sales Analysis (SQL Project)

This project analyzes transactional data for a fictional restaurant, **Urban Fork**, to uncover insights related to **revenue performance**, **customer ordering behavior**, and **menu optimization**.

The analysis covers the period from **January 2023 to March 2023** and focuses on identifying key revenue drivers, understanding customer purchase patterns, and providing data-driven recommendations to improve business performance and increase average order value.

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The primary objective of this analysis is to support data-driven decision-making for the restaurant by addressing the following key business questions:

###  Revenue & Performance

* Which menu items are the top revenue drivers?
* Which items are underperforming in terms of sales and revenue?
* How do different menu categories contribute to overall revenue?

###  Customer Behavior

* What is the average number of items per order?
* What are the most frequently ordered item combinations?
* Are customers more inclined toward standalone items or bundled purchases?

###  Menu Optimization

* Which items perform best within each category?
* Are there opportunities to promote or bundle certain items to increase sales?

###  Operational Efficiency

* What are the busiest days of the week?
* What are the peak hours of customer activity?
* How can staffing and operations be optimized based on demand patterns?

### 🚀 Business Growth

* How can the restaurant increase average order value?
* What strategies can be implemented to improve overall revenue?
